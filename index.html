Lead Capture Automation

Challenge: Manual lead entry was causing delays and data inconsistency in the sales pipeline.

Solution: Automated lead capture from Google Forms directly into Monday.com with proper categorization and instant notifications.

Workflow:

  1. Lead submits form on website
  2. Zapier captures form data
  3. Creates Monday.com item with proper formatting
  4. Assigns lead based on source and value
  5. Triggers follow-up sequence
Google Forms Zapier Monday.com

Results Achieved:

  • 95% reduction in manual data entry
  • 100% data accuracy
  • 3x faster lead response time
  • 15% increase in conversion rate

Deal Stage Notifications

Challenge: Sales team missing critical deal progressions and high-value opportunities falling through cracks.

Solution: Real-time Slack notifications when deals reach important stages, ensuring immediate team awareness.

Workflow:

  1. Deal stage changes in Monday.com
  2. Zapier detects status change
  3. Filters for high-priority stages
  4. Sends formatted Slack notification
  5. Includes deal details and next actions
Monday.com Zapier Slack

Results Achieved:

  • Zero missed high-value deals
  • 50% faster deal closure
  • Improved team collaboration
  • 25% increase in deal velocity

Smart Task Assignment
